In Assassin's Creed, you step into the shoes of Desmond Miles, a modern-day bartender with a hidden past. Through advanced technology, you relive the memories of Altair Ibn la Ahad, a skilled assassin in the time of the Third Crusade. Navigate breathtaking, historically rich cities like Jerusalem, Damascus, and Acre, where every stone tells a story. Use stealth, strategy, and acrobatics to complete dangerous missions, confront powerful enemies, and uncover secrets that could change the course of history.

Experiance a seamless blend of exploration, combat, and parkour in an immersive open world. With a deep narrative and dynamic gameplay, Assassin's Creed challenges you to master the art of stealth and strategy while unraveling a thrilling conspiracy. Are you ready to rewrite history?

In Assassin's Creed II, you continue Desmond Miles' journey as he relives the life of Ezio Auditore da Firenze, a young nobleman in Renaissance Italy. Explore the stunning cities of Florence, Venice, and Rome, navigating a world of intrigue, betrayal, and revenge. As Ezio, you hone your skills as an assassin, unraveling hidden truths and confronting powerful enemies within a vibrant, historically authentic setting.

With expanded gameplay, richer character development, and a dynamic open world, Assassin's Creed II offers thrilling missions, intricate puzzles, and exhilarating combat. Master new weapons and abilities while immersing yourself in a captivating story of family, power, and destiny. Step into the past and shape the future—your legacy awaits.

In Assassin's Creed Brotherhood, you once again step into the life of Ezio Auditore, now a Master Assassin, as he faces his greatest challenge yet: liberating Rome from the oppressive grip of the Templar Order. Explore the sprawling city of Renaissance Rome, a vibrant yet dangerous metropolis filled with ancient landmarks, bustling streets, and hidden secrets. Lead your own Brotherhood, recruiting and training new assassins to aid you in your quest for justice.

With enhanced combat, strategic team management, and a vast open world to explore, Assassin's Creed Brotherhood offers an immersive experience like never before. Build alliances, command your Brotherhood, and strike fear into your enemies. The fate of Rome—and the Assassin Brotherhood—rests in your hands.

In Assassin's Creed Revelations, you join Ezio Auditore on his final quest, as he follows in the footsteps of legendary assassin Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad. Journey to the majestic city of Constantinople, where the Templar threat looms larger than before. Unravel hidden secrets and ancient mysteries while navigating a vibrant, historically rich world filled with realistic bazaars, towering minarets, and treacherous underground tunnels.

With refined gameplay mechanics, new tools like the versatile Hookblade, and thrilling missions, Assassin's Creed Revelations offers a compelling blend of exploration, strategy, and stealth. Experience a deep, interconnected narrative that weaves the destinies of Ezio and Altaïr together, as you uncover the truth behind the Assassin Brotherhood's legacy. The end of Ezio's story is near.

In Assassin's Creed III, step into the shoes of Connor, a half-English, half-Mohawk warrior, as he fights for freedom during the American Revolution. Set in the sprawling wilderness and vibrant cities of 18th-century America, you'll experience key historical events like the Boston Tea Party and the Battle of Bunker Hill. Navigate dangerous frontiers, bustling colonial towns, and treacherous battlefields as you unravel a deep conspiracy involving the Assassins and Templars.

With dynamic combat, naval warfare, and a vast open world to explore, Assassin's Creed III offers a fresh, immersive experience. Hunt in the wild, forge alliances, and shape history as you fight for justice and liberty. Will you rise to become the hero that changes the fate of a nation?

In Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag, set sail as Edward Kenway, a brash and charismatic pirate-turned-assassin in the golden age of piracy. Explore the vast, open-world Caribbean, from bustling ports like Havana to sunken shipwrecks and hidden coves. Command your own ship, the Jackdaw, engaging in thrilling naval battles, hunting for treasure, and building a fearsome crew.

With seamless naval and land-based gameplay, Black Flag offers a rich, immersive experience full of high-seas adventure, stealth, and exploration. Customize your ship, chart your own course, and live the pirate life while unraveling the Templar conspiracy. Will you seize your destiny and carve your legend on the waves?

In Assassin's Creed Rogue, step into the boots of Shay Cormac, a former Assassin turned Templar, as he embarks on a dark journey of betrayal and redemption. Set against the backdrop of the Seven Years' War, navigate treacherous waters from the frozen North Atlantic to the bustling streets of New York, hunting down former allies who once shared your cause.

With fluid naval combat, a unique perspective as a Templar, and an expansive world to explore, Assassin's Creed Rogue offers a thrilling twist on the series’ signature gameplay. Experience the complexities of loyalty and vengeance as you pursue your mission to eliminate the Assassins—will you stay true to your new path, or question your place in the fight for power?

In Assassin's Creed Unity, immerse yourself in the tumultuous world of Revolutionary France as Arno Dorian, a young man drawn into the conflict after the tragic loss of his adoptive father. Explore the vibrant, meticulously recreated streets of Paris during one of history's most chaotic periods, from the grand Notre Dame to the bustling marketplaces of the French Revolution.

With refined parkour mechanics, a deep narrative, and the introduction of cooperative multiplayer, Unity offers a truly dynamic and immersive experience. Customize your assassin, engage in thrilling combat, and build alliances as you fight to uncover a conspiracy that threatens the heart of the revolution. Will you rise to become the hero Paris needs?

In Assassin's Creed Syndicate, take to the streets of Victorian London as twin assassins Jacob and Evie Frye, battling for control of the city's criminal underworld during the height of the Industrial Revolution. With a divided city and a ruthless Templar faction, you must fight to liberate London from corruption and injustice, using both stealth and brutal force.

Featuring innovative gameplay, including a customizable combat system, a new grappling hook, and the ability to switch between the two Frye siblings, Syndicate offers an exhilarating mix of action, strategy, and exploration. Build your gang, conquer districts, and outwit your enemies as you shape the fate of a rapidly changing world. Will you seize control of London and rewrite its future?

In Assassin's Creed Origins, journey back to Ancient Egypt during the birth of the Assassin Brotherhood. Play as Bayek of Siwa, a skilled Medjay, as he embarks on a personal quest for vengeance that intertwines with the rise of a mysterious order. Explore a vast, beautifully recreated world, from the pyramids of Giza to the bustling streets of Alexandria, and uncover the secrets of this ancient civilization.

With RPG elements, dynamic combat, and a rich narrative, Origins reinvents the series with deeper customization, immersive exploration, and an epic story that spans history’s most iconic moments. As Bayek, you'll shape the future of the Assassins while discovering the roots of their philosophy. Will you help forge a new order in a land of gods and pharaohs?

In Assassin's Creed Odyssey, step into the sandals of either Alexios or Kassandra, a mercenary in ancient Greece during the Peloponnesian War. As you journey through the stunning landscapes of Greece and beyond, make pivotal choices that shape your story and determine the fate of nations. Whether exploring the islands, battling in epic naval combat, or engaging in intense land battles, your journey is one of power, family, and destiny.

With expansive RPG elements, deep customization, and an open-world narrative, Odyssey offers a truly immersive experience. Build your legacy as a legendary warrior, navigate a world of gods and myths, and uncover ancient secrets that will alter the course of history. Will you embrace your destiny and decide the fate of Greece?

In Assassin's Creed Valhalla, embark on an epic journey as Eivor, a fierce Viking raider, navigating the harsh landscapes of 9th-century England. As your clan seeks to conquer new lands and secure a place in history, you'll battle Saxon armies, forge alliances, and build your settlement from the ground up. Your path is shaped by both brutal combat and strategic decisions that will impact the fate of your people.

With a vast open world, intense raids, and deep RPG elements, Valhalla offers an immersive Viking experience, blending exploration, storytelling, and combat. Customize your skills, lead your warriors, and experience the legends of Norse mythology. Will you carve your name into the sagas and lead your people to glory?

In Assassin's Creed Mirage, step into the shoes of Basim Ibn Ishaq, a skilled thief in the vibrant and bustling streets of 9th-century Baghdad. As Basim seeks answers about his past, he becomes entangled in a shadowy conflict between the Assassins and their enemies, leading him down a path that will shape his destiny and the future of the Brotherhood.

Focusing on stealth, parkour, and classic Assassin's Creed gameplay, Mirage delivers a more intimate, story-driven experience in a beautifully recreated historical setting. With refined combat mechanics and a narrative steeped in mystery and intrigue, Mirage brings players back to the roots of the series. Will you rise through the ranks and uncover the secrets of your fate in the heart of the Islamic Golden Age?

Assassin's Creed Shadows is an upcoming entry in the Assassin's Creed franchise, set against the backdrop of feudal Japan during the late Sengoku period. The game follows two protagonists, Naoe, a skilled shinobi assassin from the Iga Province, and Yasuke, a legendary African samurai. Their intertwined stories explore themes of honor, revenge, and the fight for a new era in a country torn by war. Players will navigate a beautifully crafted open world, from bustling towns to serene shrines, with ever-changing weather and seasons.

The dual protagonists offer distinct playstyles: Naoe relies on stealth, parkour, and assassination tools like shurikens, while Yasuke embodies the strength and martial prowess of a samurai, wielding weapons like katana and bows. Players can switch between the two characters, each with their unique progression and skills, allowing for flexible approaches to combat and missions. This game promises to combine stealth and action in a truly immersive historical setting
